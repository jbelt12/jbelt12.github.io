ABOUT ME

I am a 21-year-old student in the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland, College Park. I grew up living in Laurel, Maryland and have lived there my entire life. I have two older sisters that are 27 and 24 years old, and we are very close siblings. I played sports throughout my childhood and through high school, with basketball and baseball being the ones that I went the furthest in. I had a chance to play baseball at the collegiate level, but in the end it didn't work out. Although my competitive playing days are likely over, that still hasn't stopped my love for sports in general. My goal is to work in sports, although the details of what I would like to do have yet to be determined. I have worked in sports for about two years, being a contributing writer for a college basketball website as well as interning for the University of Maryland's Media Relations program.
